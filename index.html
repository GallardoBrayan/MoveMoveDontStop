Move,Move,Don't Stop!

Our application is Move, Move, Don't Stop. It is a web-based Motion Detection Application that helps motivate the user to complete an exercise routine by linking movement with music. When the user is actively moving in front of their web cam, our application will detect the movement and will play music. As soon as no motion is detected, the music will pause and the user's exercise routine will be put on hold. Our goal is to help motivate the user to finish the exercises by gamifying this process and the user's goal is to keep the music playing the entire duration of the routine.
